Rule of Thirds

Harry's

www.harry's.com

I think this is a pretty good example of the rule of thirds because they use break up 3 different boxes. The first box has text and a button the second has an image and the third has a text and a button as well and they are all roughly the same size. The "get started" button is lined up perfectly at the top third of the screen.

White Space and Clean Design

Airbnb

www.airbnb.com

There isn't a whole lot of white space here but I think the design is really clean. Their is a search bar up top and then a lot of open space with a button in the middle and a simple nav at the bottom.

Repitition

Ford

www.ford.com

I think Ford's website is a great example of repitition. Their iconic blue and white theme from their logo is used throughout their entire site. You can see it on the buttons, on the text and even on their navigation hamburger.
